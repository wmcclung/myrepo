About

Miami area rapper Denzel Curry emerged as an affiliate of SpaceGhostPurp's Raider Klan in 2011. Born in Carol City, FL to Bahamian and Native American parents. By the end of high school, he began penning songs that would eventually appear on his debut LP, Nostalgic 64. Curry followed the success of his debut with a double EP titled 32 Zel/Planet Shrooms. His sophomore LP "Imperial" was released in 2016 and had features from Rick Ross and Joey Bada$$. July 2018 Curry released his hard-hitting third album TA13OO to critical acclaim.
